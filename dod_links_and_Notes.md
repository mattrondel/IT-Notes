Too Many Certs show up when you try to use your CAC?

For Microsoft Edge

Start > Run > certmgr.msc > Open Certificates  - Current User (if closed) > Personal > Certificates > delete the ones you no longer need/Use

For Firefox:

Hamburger Menu> Settings > "Privacy & Security > Certificates -> View Certificates> your certificates 

in Firefox they display it as the last 6 hex delete what you don't need/use
